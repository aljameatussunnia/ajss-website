Our Institute

Building Character
Through Knowledge

AL JAMEATUS SARIMEATUS SUNNIA is a residential Islamic educational institute established under the care of Saha Ayub Educational and Charitable Trust in Hailakandi, Assam. The institute stands as a place where Islamic values are not merely taught — they are lived, practiced and internalized through every aspect of daily campus life.

Students receive a comprehensive grounding in Islamic sciences alongside structured personal development, guided by experienced scholars and educators. The residential environment ensures disciplined routines, regular worship, moral reflection and a deep connection with the Sunnah.

Special focus is given to students from orphaned, poor and underprivileged backgrounds — providing them not only education but genuine care, belonging and a pathway toward a dignified future. The institute believes that every child, regardless of circumstance, deserves access to meaningful learning, spiritual nurturing and life guidance.
